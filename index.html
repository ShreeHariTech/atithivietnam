Our Story

A Taste of India
in Vietnam

✦

Atithi — meaning 'Guest' in Sanskrit — was born from a deep reverence for Indian culinary tradition and the timeless philosophy of Atithi Devo Bhava: the guest is God.

Our kitchen brings together recipes passed down through generations — from the sweet tang of Gujarati dal to Rajasthani gravies, South Indian crêpes, and rich North Indian curries.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using the finest ingredients, to honor both our heritage and our guests.

For Jain Guests
